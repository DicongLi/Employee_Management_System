Welcome to the employee handbook. This handbook is designed to help you understand the company's policies and regulations. Please read and follow these guidelines carefully:

  • Working hours: Monday to Friday, 9:00 AM - 6:00 PM
  • Leave policy: 15 days of paid annual leave per year
  • Benefits: Employee health insurance and meal subsidies are provided
  • Code of conduct: Unacceptable behavior is strictly prohibited in the workplace
